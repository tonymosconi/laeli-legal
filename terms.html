1. What Laeli is

Laeli is an AI-powered dog training coaching app. It provides personalized, science-based, force-free training guidance from a coaching AI (the "Laeli Coach"), informed by your dog's profile and journal.

Laeli is not a substitute for a veterinarian, certified animal behaviorist, or professional trainer. For any medical concern, suspected injury, severe anxiety, aggression, or "is this normal" question, please consult your veterinarian.

2. Who can use Laeli

You must be at least 13 years old (or the minimum digital consent age in your country, whichever is higher) to use Laeli. The app is intended for dog owners and their household, not for commercial training services without explicit written agreement.

3. Subscriptions and billing

  • Free: 1 dog, 3 Laeli messages/day, 1 care reminder, 3-day journal history.
  • Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year): Up to 3 dogs, 10 Laeli messages/day, 5 care reminders, full journal, cross-session memory, apply-to-tomorrow.
  • Pro+ ($9.99/month or $79.99/year): Unlimited dogs, unlimited Laeli chat (with a fair-use cap), unlimited care reminders, AI-generated daily tasks, family sharing, priority support.

Subscriptions are billed through Apple's App Store. Apple's standard subscription terms apply: cancel any time from your Apple ID settings, auto-renews until cancelled, no refunds except as required by Apple.

4. AI coaching limits

The Laeli Coach AI:

  • Stays strictly within positive-reinforcement / LIMA / force-free training methods.
  • Will redirect medical, severe behavioral, or aggression cases to your veterinarian or a certified behaviorist (DACVB / IAABC).
  • Will refuse to discuss aversive methods (shock collars, alpha rolls, dominance theory, etc.) even if asked.
  • May make mistakes — verify any guidance that affects your dog's safety with a real professional.

You agree that you are responsible for your dog's care, training decisions, and any consequences thereof. Laeli's role is to support your decisions, not replace your judgment or your veterinarian's.
